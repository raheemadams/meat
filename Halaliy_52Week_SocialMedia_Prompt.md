## Copy Rules

**LinkedIn:** stat hero (specific number), 3-line headline (last line accent green), ~60-word body, 3 checkmark features, CTA with $ price or action phrase. Never "download" — use "start", "order", "join", "get".

**Facebook:** punchy headline max 10 words, 30–50 word body, one CTA button. Warm but direct tone.

**TikTok:** 3-line hook (last line in `#4ADE80`), middle content zone (bullets/stat cards/steps/before-after), CTA max 5 words, 5–7 hashtags over 2 lines. "halaliy.com" footer always present.

**Testimonials must include:** name, neighborhood/city in TX, one specific number (birds ordered, $ paid, lb of meat, months of orders, number of families).

**Never use:** "download", frozen meat language, pork references, alcohol references, non-halal comparisons.

**Always use:** "fresh, never frozen", "halal-certified", "Muslim butchers", "ranch-raised", community-oriented language.
